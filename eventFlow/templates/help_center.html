Frequently Asked Questions

Event Creation & Management

To create an event:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Create Event" in the navigation menu
  3. Fill in your event details
  4. Add images and ticket information
  5. Click "Create Event" to publish

You can edit your event by going to "My Events" and clicking the "Edit" button on the event you want to modify.

Tickets & Bookings

Your tickets can be found in the "My Tickets" section of your account dashboard.

Refund policies vary by event. Please check the event's refund policy or contact the event organizer.
